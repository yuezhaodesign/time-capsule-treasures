Time Capsule Treasures

Pencilbox
Year 2013
Origin Used for 5 years
Memory I used this pencil box throughout my high school years. It was a gift from my parents when I started high school, and I kept all my pens, pencils, erasers, and other stationery items in it. It helped me stay organized and prepared for my classes. I have many memories of studying, taking exams, and completing assignments with the help of this pencil box. It represents my dedication to my education and the hard work I put into my studies during those years.
