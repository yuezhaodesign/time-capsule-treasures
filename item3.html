Time Capsule Treasures

Bicycle
Year 2012
Origin My first bicycle
Memory I received this bicycle as a birthday gift from my parents when I was 8 years old. It was my first bicycle and I was so excited to learn how to ride it. I have many fond memories of riding my bicycle around the neighborhood with my friends and family. It represents a sense of freedom and independence for me, and I will always cherish the memories of my childhood adventures on this bike.
